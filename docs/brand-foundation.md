# PJBStudios.com Brand Foundation

This document captures the finalized Version 1.0 brand direction for PJBStudios.com. It defines brand positioning, tone, content rules, and visual principles for the first public website release.

## Company Name

PJBStudios

## Tagline

Ideas Into Reality.

## Brand Mission

PJBStudios exists to transform thoughtful ideas into polished digital products that solve real-world problems and deliver exceptional user experiences.

## Brand Positioning

PJBStudios is a modern software and creative technology studio.

The company designs and builds high-quality mobile applications, web platforms, business software, AI-assisted tools, and digital products.

The focus is solving real problems with thoughtful design and excellent engineering, not chasing trends.

PJBStudios.com should present the studio as a growing product studio and technology business. It should not look or sound like a generic freelance-developer portfolio, freelance landing page, or AI-startup hype site.

## North Star

"PJBStudios transforms thoughtful ideas into polished digital products that solve real-world problems."

Every product and every page should support this statement.

## Core Brand Values

- Practical Innovation
- Thoughtful Design
- Exceptional User Experience
- Simplicity
- Quality Engineering
- Continuous Improvement
- Trust
- Professionalism

## Website Goals

The first release should:

- Explain what PJBStudios is building.
- Introduce the studio's capabilities.
- Showcase selected products and projects.
- Establish credibility without exaggeration.
- Give visitors a simple way to contact or follow PJBStudios.
- Be polished enough to share publicly.
- Be suitable for Vercel deployment and connection to the PJBStudios.com domain.
- Support future growth beyond a single-page release.

## Intended Audience

The website should be approachable for:

- Visitors learning what PJBStudios is.
- Potential users of PJBStudios products.
- Potential collaborators, partners, or clients.
- People evaluating the studio's capabilities and product direction.
- Followers who want updates on current and future projects.

## Current Capabilities

PJBStudios may communicate capabilities in:

- Mobile application development.
- Web platform development.
- Business software development.
- Interactive product development.
- AI-assisted tools and digital experiences.
- Practical software systems for real user workflows.

## Possible Featured Projects

Potential projects that may eventually appear on the website include:

- PJB Fit.
- Metro Digital Pick Platform.
- To-Dos & Notes.
- AllSports GM.
- Cloud Storage App (In Development).

These are possible featured projects only. Do not imply launch status, availability, customer adoption, revenue, partnerships, or public release details unless those facts are explicitly provided.

## Company Personality

The brand should consistently communicate:

- Innovative.
- Trustworthy.
- Professional.
- Thoughtful.
- Detail-oriented.
- Approachable.
- Product-focused.

Never portray the company as arrogant, gimmicky, or driven by hype.

## Initial Tone And Personality

The site should feel:

- Premium and modern.
- Confident without sounding inflated.
- Creative but practical.
- Clear, direct, and credible.
- Ambitious while staying grounded in verified facts.
- Professional and approachable.

## Visual Identity

### Primary Logo Treatment

- `PJB` should always appear in Electric Blue.
- `Studios` should always appear in White.
- The tagline `Ideas Into Reality.` should appear beneath the logo using a muted gray.

### Visual Style

The website should communicate:

- Premium.
- Modern.
- Confident.
- Professional.
- Creative.
- Product-focused.

The design should feel like a modern technology company rather than a freelance portfolio or AI startup.

### Color Philosophy

- Background: very dark charcoal or near black.
- Primary brand accent: Electric Blue.
- Primary text: White.
- Secondary text: Soft Gray.
- Borders: subtle dark gray.
- Cards: slightly lighter than the page background.

Electric Blue should be used intentionally for:

- Buttons.
- Links.
- Focus states.
- Hover states.
- Subtle glow effects.
- Important highlights.

Avoid excessive neon, overly bright gradients, or visual clutter.

### Typography Philosophy

Typography should be:

- Modern.
- Clean.
- Highly legible.
- Premium.
- Understated.

Avoid decorative or futuristic fonts.

### Motion Philosophy

Animations should be:

- Smooth.
- Subtle.
- Purposeful.
- Polished.

Favor elegant transitions and restrained motion over flashy animations.

## Content Accuracy Rules

- Do not invent customers.
- Do not invent partnerships.
- Do not invent investors.
- Do not invent testimonials.
- Do not invent statistics.
- Do not invent launch claims.
- Do not invent revenue.
- Do not invent employees.
- Do not invent offices.
- Do not invent awards.
- Only present verified information.
- Do not imply a project is live, downloadable, funded, partnered, commercially proven, or publicly available unless that information is explicitly provided.
- Prefer careful language such as "in development", "possible featured project", or "selected work" when details are not finalized.
- Keep claims focused on PJBStudios' direction, capabilities, values, and intended website goals.

## Unresolved Decisions

- Final homepage headline and supporting copy.
- Exact Electric Blue and supporting color token values.
- Specific typeface selection.
- Contact method.
- Social links.
- Which projects will appear publicly.
- Whether the first release is one page or includes separate project pages.
- Project screenshots and artwork.
- Whether founder information will appear in the initial release.
